Frequently Asked

Common Questions

⚖
How can I solve a land dispute?+

Proper legal consultation and documentation review is required before filing a case. Advocate Khan will examine your records, identify the core dispute, and recommend the best strategy — negotiation, mediation, or litigation.

What documents are needed for property registry?+

Typically: identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN), land papers (Khasra/Khatauni), previous ownership records, original sale agreement, and an encumbrance certificate. We guide you through a complete checklist tailored to your property.

How long does a property case take?+

Duration depends on case complexity, document availability, and court procedure. Simple matters can resolve in months; complex disputes may take longer. Advocate Khan works to achieve the fastest resolution possible.

Is legal verification important before buying land?+

Absolutely. It prevents future disputes and ensures safe ownership by uncovering hidden encumbrances, litigation, or fraudulent titles. This step is essential before any property purchase.
