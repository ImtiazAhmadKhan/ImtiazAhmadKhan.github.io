Two Decades of Legal Excellence

20+ Years of Practice

Advocate Imtiaz Ahmad Khan is a highly experienced and respected property lawyer practicing in Phoolpur, Azamgarh, with more than two decades of continuous legal practice. He has built a strong reputation for handling complex property-related cases with clarity, precision, and professionalism.

With over 5,000 successfully handled cases — covering land disputes, registry work, title verification, ownership conflicts, partition cases, and documentation — his practical knowledge and courtroom experience deliver effective, tailored solutions for every client.

Known for simplifying complicated legal matters, he ensures clients fully understand their case before proceeding. His approach is built on honesty, transparency, and a strong commitment to achieving the best possible outcome.

His reputation is built not only on experience, but on the trust earned from thousands of satisfied clients who return and recommend his services to others.
